Chocolate Milkshake

Ingredients (Unordered List)

  • 2 cups milk
  • 3 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Preparation Steps (Ordered List)

  1. Pour milk into a blender.
  2. Add cocoa powder and sugar.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Add vanilla ice cream and blend again.
  5. Pour into a glass and top with whipped cream.

Cooking Terms (Definition List)

Blend:
Mix ingredients smoothly using a blender.
Whisk:
Beat ingredients with a whisk or fork.
Garnish:
Decorate a dish with cream, fruits, or herbs.
